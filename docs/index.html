only-you

Personally I prefer to focus on some I’m currently doing. This is also about viewing videos on YouTube. The problem is that there are many, many visual distractions around it: similar or recommended videos, control elements, comments. At best, they can interfere with working concentration, and at worst, they can waste time. Current bookmarklet is an attempt to deal with this. :dart:
